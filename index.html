Beneficial Ownership Registration

What is Beneficial Ownership?

Beneficial Ownership (BO) refers to the **natural person(s)** who ultimately own or control a company, even if they aren't the registered shareholders. In South Africa, CIPC regulations require companies to disclose this information to prevent financial crimes like money laundering and to ensure complete transparency.

At Laizer Consultation, we understand these regulations can be complex and time-consuming. We simplify this process, providing a seamless and professional service to ensure your Beneficial Ownership status is fully compliant with CIPC requirements. With us, you get the peace of mind that your business is operating with integrity and transparency, protecting you from non-compliance penalties.

Get Compliant for R280

Requirements:

  • ID: A clear copy of the ID or passport of the beneficial owner(s).
  • Cor14.3 Company Certificate: A copy of your company's official registration certificate.
  • Email & Phone Number: The valid contact details for the beneficial owner.
